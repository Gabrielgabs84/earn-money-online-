Top Ways to Make Money Online

1. Freelancing

Offer your skills like writing, graphic design, programming, or video editing on freelance platforms.

2. Affiliate Marketing

Promote products online and earn commissions when people buy through your referral links.

3. Blogging

Create helpful articles and earn money from ads, sponsorships, and affiliate marketing.

4. YouTube

Make videos that educate or entertain people and earn through ads and brand partnerships.

5. Selling Digital Products

Sell eBooks, online courses, templates, or other digital downloads.

6. Online Surveys

Some websites pay users for completing surveys and testing products.
